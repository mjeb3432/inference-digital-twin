## Purpose

This page packages reusable non-code artifacts that help teams turn simulation outputs into decisions.

## Included Artifacts

### 1) Assumptions Register v1

Track assumption source, confidence, owner, and review date.

### 2) Scenario Intake Worksheet

Capture objective, constraints, and planned comparison design before running scenarios.

### 3) Sensitivity Sweep Planner

Plan controlled sweeps to identify high-leverage variables.

### 4) Decision Memo Template

Convert scenario outcomes into executive-ready decisions.

### 5) Validation and QA Checklist

Ensure each published scenario/report is structurally complete and reproducible.

### 6) Risk Register

Track model, data, and operational risks with mitigation ownership.

### 7) Experiment Backlog

Prioritize future scenario experiments and validation studies.

## How to Use This Toolkit

- Start with Scenario Intake
- Run baseline + alternatives
- Use Sensitivity Sweep where uncertainty is high
- Document choice in Decision Memo
- Log residual risks in Risk Register
