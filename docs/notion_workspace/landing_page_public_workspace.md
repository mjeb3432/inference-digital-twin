## Scope and Safety Notes

V1 is a first-order planning simulator with deterministic provenance. It is designed to improve decision quality early, not replace packet-level simulation, CFD modeling, or final engineering sign-off.

For all major decisions, pair scenario outputs with domain expert review and vendor-specific validation.
