## Why This Stage Matters

Operations policy determines whether planned performance survives real-world conditions.

## Key Decision Areas

- monitoring maturity model
- maintenance model
- cooling telemetry requirements
- incident detection and response posture

## Reliability Through Operations

Higher observability and maintenance maturity typically improve practical uptime and reduce outage impact, even with identical hardware.

## Best-Practice Checklist

1. choose a monitoring stack aligned to failure detection needs
2. define maintenance policy before production scale
3. require telemetry standards for high-redundancy targets
4. track health and uptime projections as first-class outputs

## What to Compare Across Scenarios

- health score delta
- uptime projection delta
- dcim and maintenance opex impact
- warning/risk pattern differences
